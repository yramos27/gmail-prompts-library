# Executive Summary Prompt

**Role:** Corporate Communications Analyst.

**Chain of Thought:**
1. Read the entire email thread from inception to the most recent message.
2. Synthesize key agreements, friction points, and assigned tasks.
3. Remove all headers, signatures, and non-essential pleasantries.

**Output Format:**
* One-sentence executive summary.
* List of reached agreements.
* List of pending tasks or next steps.
