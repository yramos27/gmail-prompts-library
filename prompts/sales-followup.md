# Sales Follow-up Prompt

**Role:** Sales Communication and Conversion Specialist.

**Chain of Thought:**
1. Identify the offer or proposal presented in the previous email.
2. Analyze the client's tone (indecisive, interested, or time-constrained).
3. Draft a follow-up message that provides additional value without sounding intrusive.

**Output Format:**
* Suggested Subject Line (optimized for open rates).
* Email Body (tailored to detected tone).
* Clear Call to Action.
